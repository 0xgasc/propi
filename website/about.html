WHAT IS RGP?

¿QUÉ ES EL RGP?

Registro General de la Propiedad

The General Property Registry (RGP) is Guatemala's official government system for registering property ownership, transfers, and legal encumbrances. Every legitimate property transaction in Guatemala must be recorded in the RGP.

KEY FUNCTIONS:
  • • Official property ownership records
  • • Legal transfer documentation
  • • Mortgage and lien registration
  • • Property history and provenance
